A bit about me

I'm a final-year Bachelor of Computer Network Security student at UniSZA's Faculty of Informatics and Computing, currently completing my Final Year Project under the supervision of Dr. Norhidayah Muhammad.

My academic focus sits at the intersection of network security, digital forensics, and applied AI/ML — from analyzing disk images in Autopsy and memory dumps in Volatility, to building hybrid machine learning models for fraud and threat detection.

I'm especially drawn to offensive security: understanding how systems break so I can help build ones that don't. My long-term goal is to work in penetration testing and security analysis.
