Final Year Project

Vishing Detection System

AI / ML · Audio & Text Classification

A hybrid system for detecting voice phishing (vishing) in Malay-language phone calls. Combines MFCC + GaussianNB for audio analysis with Whisper speech-to-text + TF-IDF + MultinomialNB for transcript analysis, fused with a weighted scoring formula (0.8 × Text + 0.2 × Audio) to classify calls as Fraudulent, Suspicious, or Safe.

  • 96.87% accuracy
  • 32 test samples
  • Tiered keyword scoring
